Grasshoper Mouse

They are highly canabalistic and territorial and will kill other mice when in captivity

  • Scientific Name: Onychomyi Arenicola
  • Average Length: 5cm
  • Average Lifespan: 4 Years
  • Habitat: North American Deserts

They are an almost exclusively carnivorous species of a rodent who inhabit the desert plains of the southern USA and Mexico. They prey on highly venomous arthropods like scorpions and centipedes. They have evolved to deal with high levels of toxins.
